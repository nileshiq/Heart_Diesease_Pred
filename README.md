## 📂 Dataset Overview

The dataset used in this project is focused on predicting the presence of heart disease based on various patient health indicators. It contains medical and physiological attributes collected from a long term cardiovascular study, with all patient identities kept anonymous. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

The dataset consists of 14 columns, where one column represents a unique patient identifier and the remaining features describe different health conditions and test results.

### 🔑 Key Features:

- Age: Age of the patient  
- Sex: Gender of the patient  
- Chest Pain Type: Type of chest pain experienced  
- Resting Blood Pressure: Blood pressure measured at rest  
- Serum Cholesterol: Cholesterol level in mg per dl  
- Fasting Blood Sugar: Whether blood sugar is greater than 120 mg per dl  
- Resting ECG Results: Electrocardiographic results at rest  
- Max Heart Rate Achieved: Maximum heart rate during exercise  
- Exercise Induced Angina: Chest pain caused by exercise  
- Oldpeak: ST depression induced by exercise  
- Slope of ST Segment: Slope of peak exercise ST segment  
- Number of Major Vessels: Number of vessels colored by fluoroscopy  
- Thal: Thallium stress test result  

### 🎯 Target Variable:

- heart_disease_present  
  - 0 = No heart disease  
  - 1 = Heart disease present  

This dataset is widely used for classification tasks and helps in building predictive models to detect heart disease early, enabling better healthcare decisions and preventive measures. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
